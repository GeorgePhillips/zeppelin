NetHui venue

This year we’re returning to the original ‘national’ style of NetHui. It will be held on Thursday/Friday 9-10 November 2017 at the Aotea Centre at 50 Mayoral Drive in central Auckland.

On Wednesday 8 November – the day before the opening of NetHui – the Aotea Centre will also be home to the NZ Internet Research Forum. We are consulting the public to find out if people are keen to run other events on this day and around these dates. So watch this space.

Accommodation

As Auckland accommodation books out very quickly – we encourage all attendees to book a place to stay as soon as possible.

You may want to choose accommodation close to the event venue which is the Aotea Centre at 50 Mayoral Drive in central Auckland.The following sites might help you to choose accommodation that is convenient for you:

  • Booking.com
  • Trivago.co.nz
  • Airbnb.co.nz
  • Wotif.co.nz
